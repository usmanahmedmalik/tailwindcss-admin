About European Islamic Forum Netherlands

European Islamic Forum Netherlands is an excellent platform dedicated to understanding and supporting the religious, national, social, and ethical affairs of Muslims. This forum promotes a mission of love, free from sectarianism, hatred, and extremism. It serves as a unifying space to foster understanding, compassion, and cooperation among Muslims in Europe.

Our organization is committed to providing educational programs, community outreach, youth empowerment, and interfaith dialogue. We strive to create a welcoming environment for all, encouraging constructive engagement with wider society while preserving authentic spiritual and ethical identity.

Through our initiatives, we aim to nurture leadership, promote unity, and support the holistic development of individuals and communities. We believe in transparency, accountability, and collaboration as the foundation for positive change.

More Information

  • Founded: 2023
  • Location: Netherlands, serving the European Muslim community
  • Focus Areas: Education, social welfare, youth leadership, interfaith cooperation, research & advisory
  • Core Values: Unity, compassion, integrity, inclusivity, and service
