Welcome

European Islamic Forum Netherlands is an excellent platform dedicated to understanding and supporting the religious, national, social, and ethical affairs of Muslims. This forum promotes a mission of love, free from sectarianism, hatred, and extremism. It serves as a unifying space to foster understanding, compassion, and cooperation among Muslims in Europe.

We facilitate educational development, civic participation, inter‑community dialogue, youth empowerment, and strategic collaboration—anchored in integrity, responsibility, and shared moral values.

Our work encourages constructive engagement with wider society, strengthening cohesion while preserving authentic spiritual and ethical identity. We nurture leadership that rejects polarization and embraces principled service.

Explore our initiatives to learn how you can contribute to a forward‑looking, inclusive European Muslim presence rooted in compassion, excellence, and unity.

Mission & Vision

We envision a confident, cooperative European Muslim community advancing the common good—embodying mercy, justice, ethical excellence, and cultural contribution.

Core Ethos

Non‑sectarian, principled, cooperative; rejecting hatred, extremism, and division; amplifying constructive voices.

Strategic Focus

Education, civic responsibility, social wellbeing, youth leadership, ethical governance, research & advisory.

Human Development

Elevating character, literacy, service, emotional resilience, and collaborative problem solving.

Foundational Principles

  • Unity above factionalism; mutual respect across diverse schools of thought.
  • Integrity, transparency, and accountability in structure and output.
  • Evidence‑informed programming aligned with ethical objectives.
  • Holistic youth empowerment—spiritual, intellectual, and civic.
  • Collaborative partnerships with institutions and civil society.

Impact Pathways

We translate vision into measurable outcomes through curriculum support, advisory forums, leadership workshops, mentorship networks, research briefs, strategic convenings, and digital knowledge resources.
