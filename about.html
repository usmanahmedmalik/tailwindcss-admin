About Us

Cultivating Flourishing Muslim Communities Across Europe

European Islamic Forum Netherlands convenes scholars, professionals, youth and community partners around a shared mission of ethical leadership, civic engagement, and compassionate service.

Who We Are

Founded in 2011, the European Islamic Forum Netherlands is a platform for collaboration, guidance, and impact-driven initiatives serving Muslim communities throughout the continent. We champion intellectual growth, spiritual refinement, and social responsibility through a network of strategic partners and local chapters.

Our team brings together educators, policy specialists, counselors, researchers, and artists who work in unison to shape inclusive programming and cultivate principled leadership. We believe that flourishing communities are built on justice, equity, empathy, and a fearless commitment to the common good.

What drives us

The Forum operates on a values-based framework: prophetic ethics, scholarly rigor, and collaborative problem-solving. Programs span civic education, youth mentorship, research translation, and culturally responsive wellbeing services, ensuring every community can access relevant resources and trusted guidance.

Strategic Pillars

  • Scholarship and research translated into practical community support.
  • Leadership pathways for youth and professionals grounded in service.
  • Inclusive civic engagement cultivating trust with institutions and neighbours.
  • Holistic wellness resources that honour faith, identity, and mental health.

A Decade of Collective Progress

2011 — Founding Summit

Community leaders convened to articulate a unified voice for European Muslims and formed the Forum with a charter rooted in mercy and justice.

2015 — Youth & Civic Labs

Launched innovation labs pairing scholars with young professionals to develop civic engagement toolkits and leadership retreats.

2019 — Wellbeing Consortium

Established cross-country wellbeing resources, integrating counseling, mentorship, and faith-informed mental health frameworks.

2023 — Digital Knowledge Hub

Rolled out multilingual digital libraries, podcasts, and research spotlights to connect communities with expert guidance worldwide.
